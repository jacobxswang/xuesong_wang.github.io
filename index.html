About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen). My current focus is diffusion-aided semantic communication systems—especially receiver-driven protocols with adaptive requests—and learning-augmented physical-layer network coding (PNC) with practical signal-processing constraints.

  • Current: Postdoctoral Researcher, CUHK-Shenzhen (May 2024 – Present)
  • Ph.D.: Computer Applied Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), 2017 – 2023
  • B.Eng.: Communication Engineering (Elite Program), Beijing Jiaotong University, 2013 – 2017
  • Location: Shenzhen, China

News

  • 2024–Present Diffusion-aided semantic communication: privacy–utility trade-off; bandwidth-efficient adaptive requests; incremental semantics.
  • 2017–2023 DNN-based physical-layer network coding: end-to-end learning + model-based PHY pipeline, with benchmarking and practical impairments under wireless/visible-light communications.
