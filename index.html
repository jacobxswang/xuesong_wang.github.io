About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen).
My recent research focuses on semantic communication, including receiver-driven protocols, end-to-end evaluation, and security. I also study AI for wireless and visible-light communications and network coding, using generative and multimodal models when they provide measurable gains under realistic system constraints. Previously, I worked on DNN-based physical-layer network coding (PNC), with simulation and USRP implementation.

  • Current: Postdoctoral Researcher, CUHK-Shenzhen (May 2024 – Present)
  • Ph.D.: Computer Applied Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), 2017 – 2023
  • B.Eng.: Communication Engineering (Elite Program), Beijing Jiaotong University, 2013 – 2017
